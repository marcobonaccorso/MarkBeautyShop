The Mark's barber shop was born in Rome in 2021 from the intuition and dream of its creator Marco Bonaccorso. The shop is located in Via Cipro 100, a stone's throw from San Pietro and the Vatican Museums and consists of two floors: on the ground floor there is the men's section and on the upper floor there is the women's section. Male section: over the years it has become a real point of reference for grooming lovers, The Mark's barber shop offers beard and hair services of the highest quality. Every detail is taken care of to accompany you on an authentic cutting and shaving experience in an environment with a formal and vintage tone. All the services offered represent the perfect combination of the art of Italian shaving and the best techniques from American and English barbershops. The Mark's barber shop also offers its customers a complete line of Bullfrog products and accessories that help make grooming a real old school cult.
